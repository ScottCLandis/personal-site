Sotheby’s International Realty Anthology App
Sales Enablement App | iPad | Adobe DPS
The Sotheby’s International Realty (SIR) Anthology app is a sales enablement application built on Adobe’s Digital Publishing Suite Platform. The app provides affliates of SIR to tell the story of the realty brand and entice potenial home sellers to work with SIR.
